Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Who We Are

LuminaPath ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered social media automation platform operated by LuminaPath Inc., based in Vancouver, BC, Canada. We help creators and businesses publish authentic content across multiple social media platforms.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@luminapath.ca.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account details: name, email address, password
  • Billing information processed securely through Stripe (we never store card numbers)
  • Brand profile content: writing samples, brand voice data, social media content you upload
  • Messages you send to our support or BEACON chatbot

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Information from social media platforms

When you connect a social media account (X, YouTube, TikTok, etc.), we receive OAuth tokens and any data you authorize. We use this only to post content on your behalf and analyse your account performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our platform
  • To train your personal Brand Voice Engine (your data is never used to train models for other customers)
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send you product updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Retention

Brand Voice Pattern — stored indefinitely in our secure database. This is the core of our service. Even if you cancel, your pattern is preserved so you can resubscribe without starting over.

Generated Posts — retained for 90 days, then automatically purged.

Raw Uploads — videos and images you upload count toward your storage limit and can be deleted by you at any time.

Account Data — retained for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days after cancellation, then deleted on request.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with:

  • Service providers — Supabase (database), DigitalOcean (hosting), Stripe (payments), ElevenLabs (voice synthesis), Anthropic (AI processing). All bound by data processing agreements.
  • Social media platforms — only what is necessary to post content you approve.
  • Law enforcement — only when required by law or to protect our users and platform.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We use analytics cookies to understand how our platform is used. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for optional data uses

To exercise any of these rights, email support@luminapath.ca. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures including TLS encryption in transit, encrypted storage at rest, and strict access controls. However, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication when available.

9. Children

LuminaPath is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you by email or in-app notice at least 14 days before material changes take effect. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
